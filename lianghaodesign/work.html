外滩SOHO / Bund SOHO

上海 / Shanghai_2013

项目业主：SOHO中国
占地面积：22462平方米
建筑面积：189733平方米
项目时间：2013.06
合作方：    德国gmp建筑设计

外滩SOHO是上海外滩过去60年间唯一的整体新建项目，也将是最后一个新建项目。被称为“外国建筑博览群”的外滩，在上海具有极其特殊的地位，建成后的外滩soho将汇集高档商业楼及写字楼，成为外滩独特的一道风景。良好为此项目设计了项目Logo及环境引导系统。
经典永恒的黑色钻石作为设计灵感，为高耸冷峻的建筑环境增添时尚感与年轻活力是良好的目标，设计前期经过了无数轮的概念方案探讨，最终的结果由于多方意见被磨去了很多光芒，但仍旧体现了良好追求的品质。

Client name: SOHO China
Site area: 22462 sqm
GFA: 189733sqm
Start time:2013.06
Collaborator:gmp architecture, Beijing

BUND SOHO is the only one new complex project in Shanghai BUND area in the past of 60 years. It will be also a last new project in this area. Bund is named as “exhibition center of skyscrapers from all over the world”. BUND SOHO will be a complex building with high classic commercial space and office, which will be very impressive as a new image in this the special area.
Lianghao design team finished the signage system design for BUND SOHO.
The concept is from black diamond -sharp, cold and modern which makes the building less serious, more fashionable and fresh. Unfortunately after several round of concept discussion and adjustment, our original concept idea was changed a lot but we still keep the final one with “Lianghao” quality.

南岸一号商务中心 / South Bank NO.1 Center

北京 / Beijing_2013.08

项目业主：北京万瑞投资管理有限公司
项目时间：2013.08
占地面积：700000平方米

高碑店通惠河畔产业区中的南岸一号商务中心，位于通惠河南岸，复古的宫殿风格建筑金碧辉煌。良好为南岸一号重新设计了项目标志并发展了整个引导系统。标识设计的灵感来自于古代花窗。格子、窗口、色彩是主要的设计元素，设计从功能出发，以现代人作为服务对象，平衡现代办公与传统文化关系，经过了与客户的不断探讨与推敲，最终希望呈现出标识与建筑合理的关系并形成有特色的品牌形象。

Client: Beijing Wang Rui Investment and Management, Co.Ltd
Site area: Site area: 700000m2
Start time: Start time: 2013.08

South Bank No.1 center is located in the tong hui river industrial park in Gao Bei Dian,east of Beijing.
It will be office building. Chinese traditional style makes this project very impressive. Lianghao design has worked from the LOGO design the whole wayfinding system since Aug of 2013. The concept comes from Chinese traditional windows graphic.
We took the grid,window and color from tradition as the main design elements.
Considering the function, balancing the traditional looking with modern office function, we deliver our design, functional, modern, colorful and impressive.

西海48号文化创意园区 / Xihai 48

北京 / Beijing_2014

项目业主：北京永康新物业管理有限责任公司
项目时间：2014.02
占地面积：6000平米

西海湖畔，根基于什刹海独特的文化土壤。寺庙到工厂到时尚办公区，几经转身痕迹宛在。改造的厂房，朴素端庄，内部空间高大宽敞，会呼吸的房子和好日子是这里的名片。人与自然的和谐让这里随处充满生活的趣味和滋味。>br> 将自然的美引入设计之中，用细致和亲切的形式欢迎来到院子的朋友，良好作为西海48策划及创意设计合作团队，完整的园区形象工程正在一步步实现。

Client name: Beijing Yong Kang Xin Property, Co, Ltd
Start time: 2014.02
Site area：6000m2

Xihai 48 cultural and creative park beside the Xihai lake, growing up from the culture of Shi Chai Hai area. It is featured as an old temple, industrial factory spaces and different way of good life. The harmonious atmosphere of nature and human makes it unique and impressive.
Lianghao design ‘brings’ nature into Xihai 48 VI design which shows hospitality to all friends when visiting this park. The whole VI design will be completed step by step.

喜玛拉雅艺术中心 / Himalayas Center

上海 / Shanghai_2013.04

项目业主：上海证大集团
项目时间：2013.04
占地面积：29000平方米
建筑面积：155hm2
合作方：    建筑设计 法国AS

由日本建筑大师矶崎新设计的前卫建筑，投资方斥资20亿人民币打造，是目前上海最大的商业文化中心。由五星级酒店和精品酒店、当代艺术馆、剧场、商业中心、创意办公室五大业态共同组成。
良好为最具特色的巨型有机剧场部分设计引导标识。建筑师采用EOS方法（进化结构最优手法）创造的巨大的有机造型，进入其间好像置身巨大的溶洞之中。天光、舞台、树林。。。都是我们的灵感。为达到艺术性和功能性的兼具，良好创造出用带有引导信息的投影光斑在有机的建筑表面游走，形成多变而奇妙的视觉效果这一概念得到业主建筑方的一致认可，并在此基础深化出最后的方案。

Client: Shanghai Chia Tai Group
Start time: 2013.04
Site area: 29000 sqm
GFA: 155hm2
Collaborator: Architectural Studio (AS), Shanghai.

Himalayas Center is designed by Arata Isozaki, Japanese famous front guard architect. It is the biggest commercial cultural center, composed of 5 star hotel, contemporary art gallery, theatre, shopping center, creative offices.
LiangHao provide signage system design for the big organic shape art space which makes you feel walking in a karst cave. Our concept is from sunshine, stage, forest… to make signage walking in this space. We decide to use lights as signage（projection） to give more floating elements in this big “cave”. The concept convince all the client and we carry it on till design development stage.
