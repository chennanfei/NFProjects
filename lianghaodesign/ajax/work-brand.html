西海48号文化创意园区 / Xihai 48

北京 / Beijing_2013.08

项目业主：北京永康新物业管理有限责任公司
项目时间：2013.8

西海湖畔，跟基于什刹海独特的文化土壤。寺庙到工厂时尚办公区，几经转身痕迹犹在。 改造的厂房，朴素端庄，内部空间高大宽敞，会呼吸的房子和好日子是这里的名片。 人与自然的和谐让这里随处充满生活的趣味和滋味。
将业主对于原子关于好日子好生活的想法注入logo字体的设计之中，又要体现环境、建筑以及文化创意园区的特点， 良好的设计得到业主的高度认可，并展开了一系列后续设计。

Client：Beijing Yong Kang Xin Property, Co, Ltd
Start time: 2013.08

West Lake, Shichahai unique culture rooted in the soil. Temple to the factory to the fashion office, and turned several times in the traces Wan. Transform of the plant, simple and dignified, tall and spacious interior space, the house and breathing is a good day here card. Harmony between man and nature and make it fun and taste everywhere lift.
The owners of the yard on a good day a good idea of injecting life logo font designs, but also reflect the characteristics of the environment, architecture, and culture and creative park, good design has been highly recognized by the owners, and launched a series of follow-up design.

闺房时尚店 / Boudoir Nail’s Salon

上海 / Shanghai_2013.04

良好设计 / Lianghao Design

北京 / Beijing_2013.5

项目时间：2013.5

“良好“就像这个词本身给人的清晰正面的印象，我们通过红色、有秩序、精确又变化无穷的线段组合产生空间、字体、图案的意向，把我们设计理想及设计手法表现出来，通过设计强化这个词给人的感受。

Start time: 2013.05

“Lianghao”, the words in Chinese gives you a pure and positive image. In order to highlight the feeling of the word "Lianghao" deliver, we decide to use short changing lines with pure red color to make spaces, font, and graphic image.

闺房时尚店 / Boudoir Nail’s Salon

上海 / Shanghai_2013.04

项目时间 2013.04

“Boudoir（闺房）”是上海一家提供美甲服务的时尚小店，这个名字私人而神秘，巴黎风格的家庭式陈设营造了别具一格的空间体验。VI设计就是店本身给人感受的延伸，渐变的镭射效果、迷幻的电子色彩、跳跃的动感图案 形成既复古又活泼的感觉，正是客户想要的味道。

Start time: 2013.04

“Boudoir”is a nails beauty salon. This name sounds private and mysterious. The interior layout with Paris style makes this salon special experience.
VI design is just an extend from the feeling which “Boudoir”wants to make. Laser effect,electrical illusion,jumping image make this space taste like vintage but still fresh.
