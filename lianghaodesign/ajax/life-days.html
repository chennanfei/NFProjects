泡泡包 / Bombelouva

北京 / Beijing_2013

设计师：江小飞 (波兰)
摄影师：小卢（德国）

偶然的一个想法, 泡泡防水信封为何不能通过改造变成你的背包？我们的建筑师、产品设计师Filip Galuszka给彩色的泡泡防水信封，加上了建筑材料pvc软管，让这一款独特的包包可以背在你的身上。
信封颜色和pvc软管颜色可以任你喜好自由搭配。背着它你可以每天游走城市亦或参加各种音乐节。包包尺寸：34x25cm

Designer：Filip Galuszka (Poland)
Photographer：Lucas Zimmermann (Germany)

There are ideas that are born by accident, naturally, as the need of the situation. So it was like that with our concept of shoulder bags, when ordinary bubble envelope was adapted to simple hand bag. Intuitive cut handle, old stamps with umbrella, wallet and notebook inside . After 3 days, it turned out that this simple envelope is so handy and powerful that it should devote more attention.
This is how we created Bombelouva – bag designed on the basis of aluminum bubble envelope and insulating shoulder strap.
