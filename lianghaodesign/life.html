安妮画展 / Anni ink art exhibition

北京 / Beijing_2014.03

安妮，自由视觉艺术家，《观墨觉品》个人首展
2014年3月21日在西海48文化创意园区毗卢殿拉开序幕。良好团队参与布展及后勤支持工作。

Annie, an visual artist. The first private exhibition took place in Xihai 48 cultural and Creative Park in 21st Mar.2014. Lianghao team helps on the exhibition hall set up and logistic support.

禅修 / Meditation trip

西安 / Xi’an_2013.05

放下都市的繁华，放下手中的工作，回归清静，让你的心安静的和自己待上几日。对于中国文化，良好想说了解是理解的开始

To leave the big busy and noisy city, leave your work for a while, stay with heart for few days. For Chinese traditional culture, we want to say trial is the beginning of understanding.

以物易物市集 / Swap meet fair

一个闲置物品交换、分享、捐赠的公益平台。
每月第三个星期六下午在西海48文化创意园区举办。
一年半的时间里，良好义务为每一期活动进行策划及视觉推广

This is a place for second-hand stuff exchange, share and donate. The third Saturday of every month, this swap meeting fare will take place in Xihai48 park.As a volunteer, in the past one and half year, Lianghao team works on its organization and advertisement.

易书屋 / book exchange library

北京 / Beijing_2013

关于一本书的旅行。闲置书籍交换分享平台，由良好与西海48联合策划推广。

It is a trip about your books. Unused books can share in this library. Lianghao team works with Xihai 48 team together make it happen.
