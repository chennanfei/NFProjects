About Us

Started in March 2013, G-Wearables is a tech start-up focused on fashionable and versatile electronic wearables. We are a united team with a passion for creativity and innovation, focusing on wearable electronic devices, technology and equipment R&D. Believing that all wearable electronics should gather information naturally and act smartly, our core competence lies in innovatively combining technology, engineering and design.

Starting from just a few members, G-Wearables is now a team of 19 located in both San Francisco and Beijing. Made up of coffee-loving engineers, experienced designers, fashionistas, and a stellar marketing team, all with an average of 10 years of experience in their respective fields, we are excited to introduce our products to the world.
