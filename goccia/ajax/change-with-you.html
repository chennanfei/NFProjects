关于我们About me

Starting from just a few members, G-Wearables is now a team of 19 located in both San Francisco and Beijing. Made up of coffee-loving engineers, experienced designers, fashionistas, and a stellar marketing team, all with an average of 10 years of experience in their respective fields, we are excited to introduce our products to the world. G-wearables创始人是半导体及LED照明领域知名专家，Goccia的研发团队由海外归国博士、资深风险基金管理人、经验丰富的产业工程师以及优秀研发工程师组成。我们秉承着“大道至简“的设计理念，平衡技术与美学、科技与设计，希望我们的产品能够服务于大众，改变人们的生活。

Brand story品牌故事

Started in March 2013, G-Wearables is a tech start-up focused on fashionable and versatile electronic wearables. We are a united team with a passion for creativity and innovation, focusing on wearable electronic devices, technology and equipment R&D. Believing that all wearable electronics should gather information naturally and act smartly, our core competence lies in innovatively combining technology, engineering and design. 2013年，Goccia在实验室里有了最初的雏形，在可穿戴大佬林立的今天，Goccia的工程师们坚持着可穿戴——“要么美丽，要么看不见”这样极致的态度，打造了一款世界上最小的活动追踪器。Goccia所有零件浑然一体，极简的设计、时尚的基因、精良的材质、尖端的科技、易用的操作，正可谓，对品质我们无法妥协，才让Goccia从每个角度都闪耀出TA的光芒——精致、优雅、极致、灵动。
